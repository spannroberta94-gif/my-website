Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about YqoKey hardware wallets

What makes YqoKey wallets more secure than software wallets?

YqoKey wallets store your private keys in an isolated secure element, completely offline. This means your keys never touch internet-connected devices, protecting you from remote hacking attempts, malware, and phishing attacks that software wallets are vulnerable to.

Which cryptocurrencies does YqoKey support?

Our wallets support Bitcoin, Ethereum, and over 10,000 other cryptocurrencies and tokens across multiple blockchains. We continuously add support for new assets through regular firmware updates.

What happens if I lose my YqoKey wallet?

You can recover your funds using your 24-word recovery phrase on any compatible wallet. Your crypto isn't stored on the physical device - it's on the blockchain. The wallet just securely stores your private keys.

How often should I update my wallet's firmware?

We recommend updating whenever you receive a notification about new firmware. Updates typically include security enhancements, new features, and additional cryptocurrency support. Our app makes updating simple and secure.

Can I use my YqoKey wallet with multiple devices?

Yes, you can connect your wallet to multiple computers or mobile devices. The wallet itself remains the single source of truth for your private keys, regardless of how many devices you connect it to.

What's the difference between your three wallet models?

The Biometric Wallet features fingerprint authentication, the Card Wallet is ultra-thin like a credit card, and the Flex Wallet offers the most features and screen size. All provide the same military-grade security but cater to different usage preferences.

How do I know my YqoKey wallet is genuine?

Each wallet has a unique holographic seal and verifiable cryptographic signature. Our app will automatically detect and warn you if the device appears counterfeit during setup.

Can I stake cryptocurrencies using an YqoKey wallet?

Yes! Our wallets support staking for many Proof-of-Stake coins. You can securely delegate your assets while keeping your keys protected in the hardware wallet.

What's your warranty policy?

All YqoKey wallets come with a 2-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects. The warranty doesn't cover physical damage or loss of the device. Your crypto assets remain secure regardless of warranty status.

How do I get support if I have issues?

We offer 24/7 support through live chat, email, and our extensive knowledge base. All support requests are handled by our in-house security experts, never outsourced.
