Product Details

The YqoKey Card Wallet is the world's thinnest cryptocurrency hardware wallet, designed to fit seamlessly in your physical wallet. Despite its slim profile, it offers uncompromising security with a built-in secure element and intuitive touch controls.

With no battery required for NFC operation, the Card Wallet provides true cold storage with maximum security. It's perfect for those who want to keep their digital assets secure without carrying a separate device.

The E-paper display provides clear visibility for transaction verification, and the wallet supports over 700 cryptocurrencies and tokens.
